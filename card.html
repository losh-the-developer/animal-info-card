Elephant

There’s an easy way to tell the African elephants apart from their Asian cousins – their ears!

  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Length: Asian elephant: 5,5 – 6,5 m
  • Gestation period:18 – 22 months
  • Lifespan: 60 – 70 years
  • Height: 2,7 m

There are three different species of elephant – the African Savannah elephant, the African Forest elephant and the Asian elephant.
